Swords-Spears-Horses

(a medieval rock-paper-scissors game)

You are a general on a field of a medieval battle.

You will fight with your opponent by choosing a regiment to pit against their choice. You can choose between a sword infantry, a spear infantry and cavalry.

Remember, sword infantry always wins against a spear infantry, spear infantry always wins against cavalry and cavalry always wins against sword infantry. Choosing the same regiment results in a tie.

You will fight until one of you wins 10 times.

Choose your regiment!
